Training

"Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn." - Benjamin Franklin

Karen Baldwin is a National Champion trainer with over 25 years of experience. She believes in developing a partnership between horse and rider.

Although her passion is the Arabian breed, she enjoys the challenge of working with any horse who can benefit from her experience. She welcomes all breeds and levels in for training.

Karen is experienced in working with youngsters who are ready to be backed for the first time, horses who may need to be tuned up before the riding season starts, and in putting the finishing touches on the national caliber horse. As she works with your horse, you are encouraged to take a weekly lesson to experience the progression of your horse's training.

Training methods vary for each individual horse to encourage maximum acquisition of both skills and strength. Training includes, but is not limited to, riding, jogging and/or bitting rigs, long lining or cardio fitness workouts. The amount a horse is worked is determined by age, health, ability and the goals by the owner.

Baldwin Stables offers several different training options to fit the needs of the horse and the owner:

Option 1 - 1 time per week training
Option 2 - 3 times per week training (Includes 1 lesson per week)
