Lesson Program

Baldwin Stables offers a multi-faceted lesson program, catering to multitude of riders

We welcome all level of riders. From beginners, with dreams of experiencing their first rocking horse canter; to juniors or amateurs, with goals of national competition. At Baldwin Stables, clients learn to ride on safe, experienced lesson horses. As your skills and confidence develop, you advance to more forward moving steeds. We provide a nurturing environment, with just the right amount of challenge. This allows our riders to be the best they can be.

We strive to help you develop goals and work hard to achieve them. The results speak for themselves.

  • Western seat equitation/pleasure
  • Dressage

  • Balanced seat/Centered riding
