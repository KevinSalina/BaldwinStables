Our barn is family friendly, welcoming horse owners of all ages. The stable is open to boarders every day. We encourage you to spend time with your horses; whenever your schedule allows.

Board Includes

  • Daily individual or group turnout - weather permitting
  • New York state hay - 3 times per day
  • Supplements and medication administered
  • Blanketing - as needed

Amenities

  • Stalls: 17 inside, 11 outside with paddocks attached
  • Indoor arena (130x70), Outdoor arena (170x90)
  • 60ft. Round pen
  • 3 Grooming stalls
  • Hot and cold wash rack (year round)
  • Individual tack lockers
  • Heated lounge with kitchen and restroom facilities
  • Owners and trainer live on the premises
  • Several grass areas for hand grazing
  • In-barn veterinary service - Salmon Brook Veterinary
  • In-barn farrier - Ted Trask
  • Access to Cockaponsett State forest - miles of trails

Though we offer in-barn veterinary and farrier services, owners are welcome to use their own trusted equine service providers.

Baldwin Stables gives you piece of mind, knowing your horse is being cared for by our devoted and experienced staff.

We invite you to come meet our staff and feel the Baldwin Stables experience in person!.
